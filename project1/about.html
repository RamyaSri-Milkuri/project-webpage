Why Organic?

ORGANIC FRUIT IS OUR PASSION

Our main objective is to provide people with a variety of healthy and flavourful foods that are chemical free. So they enjoy flavours of the of packed food without any fear. We ensure our products are 100% organic by following a chemical free practice through the whole production cycle - from cultivation to processing.

Organic farming encourages food production that nurtures our soil through the absence of pesticides and the presence of rich compost. The inherent commitment of organic farming to crop rotations, living soil, rural enterprise, pure water, and sustainable agriculture is a critical step toward protecting our environment and our individual health. In buying organic, you provide a marketplace for growers who have made the future of our planet a top priority.

Frequently Asked Questions

01. What is Organic food?

Organic food is grown without the use of synthetic chemicals, such as human-made pesticides and fertilizers, and does not contain genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Organic foods include fresh produce, meats, and dairy products as well as processed foods

02.Why choose Organic food?

Eating organic reduces the amount of chemicals in your diet namely persistent pesticides. The use of insecticides, fungicides, fertilizers and weedkillers are strictly monitored in organic food production. Organic farming produces healthy food without the use of toxic pesticides.

03.Why should you opt for Organic food?

Choosing organic can reduce the risk of exposure to potentially harmful chemicals used in conventional agriculture. Environmental Benefits: Organic farming methods often focus on soil health, biodiversity, and reduced environmental impact.

04.Is organic 100% natural?

Organic is always natural and non-GMO, but the opposite cannot be said.
